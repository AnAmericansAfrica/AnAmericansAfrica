### [Cairo, Egypt 1950 – The Opera Square](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=awa6tSHXeeA)
This archival footage shows the Opera Square in Cairo where the Khedivial Opera House once sat. The footage demonstrates what a daily walk in Cairo may have been like for Lydia during her time in Egypt.
### [Life or Death](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0342512/)
This post-revolution film by director Kamal al-Shaikh was shot on the streets of Cairo in 1954. It addresses the journey of a girl attempting to get medication for her sickly father who instead receives poison.
